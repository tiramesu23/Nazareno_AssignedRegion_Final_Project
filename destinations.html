Popular Destinations

Maria Cristina Falls

This majestic twin waterfall in Iligan City is one of the most iconic landmarks in Mindanao. It powers the Agus Hydroelectric Plant and offers breathtaking views of nature.

Location: Iligan City, Lanao del Norte

Dahilayan Adventure Park

Home to Asia's longest dual zipline, Dahilayan offers thrilling rides and scenic mountain views for adventure seekers.

Location: Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon

Camiguin Island

Camiguin Island, known as the "Island Born of Fire," is a volcanic paradise in Northern Mindanao. It features pristine white-sand beaches, majestic waterfalls, and historic sites like the Sunken Cemetery and hot springs.

Location: Camiguin Province, Northern Mindanao

White Island

An uninhabited sandbar off Camiguin's coast, known for its crystal-clear waters and perfect view of Mt. Hibok-Hibok.

Location: Camiguin Province

Tinago Falls

Nestled in a deep ravine in Iligan City, Tinago Falls is a breathtaking natural wonder with turquoise waters and a refreshing atmosphere perfect for swimming and photography.

Location: Iligan City, Lanao del Norte

Cagayan de Oro River

Known as the whitewater rafting capital of the Philippines, this river offers exciting rafting adventures and scenic landscapes that attract thrill-seekers from all over.

Location: Cagayan de Oro City, Misamis Oriental
